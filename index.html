What is H3lix client?

H3lix client is an internal Minecraft Bedrock hack client, or as we like to call it, a utility mod. The download page can be found here.

H3lix client also has a forum which can be found here. This forum can be used to post questions, helpful tips, and more such as tips for the devs on how to improve, or maybe even just request a feature. The forum is used for anything related to this client.
